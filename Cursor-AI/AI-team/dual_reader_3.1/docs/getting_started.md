# Getting Started with Dual Reader 3.1

Welcome to Dual Reader 3.1! This guide will help you get started with reading ebooks in multiple languages.

## What is Dual Reader?

Dual Reader 3.1 is a cross-platform ebook reader that displays original and translated text side-by-side. It helps you read ebooks in languages you're learning or don't fully understand by showing both the original text and its translation simultaneously.

## First Steps

### Step 1: Import Your First Book

**On Mobile (Android/iOS):**
1. Launch the Dual Reader app
2. Tap the **"Import Book"** button (floating action button with a plus icon) in the bottom right corner
3. Select an EPUB or MOBI file from your device storage
4. The book will be added to your library automatically
5. You'll see a confirmation message when the import is complete

**On Web:**
1. Open Dual Reader in your web browser
2. Click the **"Import Book"** button
3. **OR** simply drag and drop an EPUB or MOBI file directly onto the library screen
4. The book will be imported and added to your library
5. A success message will appear when the import completes

**Supported File Formats:**
- EPUB (.epub) - Most common ebook format
- MOBI (.mobi) - Kindle format

**Tips:**
- Make sure your ebook files are not corrupted
- Large books may take a moment to import
- You can import multiple books at once

### Step 2: Open a Book

1. Find your imported book in the library
2. Tap on the book card to open it
3. The reader will open:
   - **If you've read it before**: Automatically resume from your last position
   - **If it's new**: Start at the beginning (page 1)

**What You'll See:**
- Two panels showing original and translated text
- Reading controls at the bottom
- Bookmark button at the top

### Step 3: Configure Translation

**Initial Setup:**
1. Tap the **Settings** icon (gear ‚öôÔ∏è) in the top right corner of any screen
2. Scroll to the **Translation** section
3. Tap **Translation Language** and select your preferred language from the dropdown
   - Choose from 50+ languages including Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, and many more
4. Toggle **Auto-translate** to ON (recommended)
   - This automatically translates pages as you read
5. Optionally enable **Synchronized Scrolling** to keep both panels aligned

**Translation Tips:**
- First translation may take a few seconds (requires internet)
- Subsequent pages use cached translations and load faster
- Translations are saved locally for offline reading
- You can change the translation language anytime in Settings

## Understanding the Reader Interface

### Dual-Panel Display

The reader shows two panels displaying the same content in different languages:

**Portrait Mode** (Mobile/Tablet Vertical):
- **Top Panel**: Original text from the book
- **Bottom Panel**: Translated text
- Panels are stacked vertically for easy reading on smaller screens

**Landscape Mode** (Mobile/Tablet Horizontal or Desktop):
- **Left Panel**: Original text from the book
- **Right Panel**: Translated text
- Panels are side-by-side for easy comparison

**How to Switch:**
- Rotate your device to automatically switch layouts
- The app adapts the layout based on screen orientation

### Reader Controls

**Show/Hide Controls:**
- Tap anywhere on the reading area to toggle controls visibility
- Controls appear/disappear smoothly

**Top Bar** (when controls are visible):

## Basic Reading Tips

### Getting Started Tips

1. **First Time Setup:**
   - Choose your translation language in Settings
   - Select a comfortable theme (Dark is great for night reading)
   - Adjust font size to your preference (16pt is default)

2. **Reading:**
   - **Swipe or use arrow buttons** to turn pages
   - **Tap the screen** to toggle controls visibility
   - **Use bookmarks** to mark important pages for quick reference
   - **Rotate your device** to switch between portrait and landscape layouts
   - **Enable synchronized scrolling** for easier reading (Settings ‚Üí Translation)

3. **Customization:**
   - **Adjust settings** to customize your reading experience
   - Try different themes for different lighting conditions
   - Experiment with font sizes and margins
   - Export your settings to backup or transfer to another device

4. **Performance:**
   - First translation may take a moment (requires internet)
   - Subsequent pages load faster (uses cached translations)
   - Large books may take time to load initially
   - Adjust font size if pages load slowly

5. **Offline Reading:**
   - Books work offline once imported
   - Cached translations are available offline
   - Read online first to cache translations, then read offline
